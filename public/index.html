Welcome to Imperium!

Imperium is a web based idle game that draws inspiration from games like "cookie clicker".

The goal of the game is simply to generate as much money as possible and that is done by buying one or more entities that will automatically generate revenue for you.

In order to continue, press the button below and then pick your empire! Different empires have different bonuses so pick wisely!

You have now chosen an empire, press the economics tab to buy your first worker which will generate revenue for you!


Civilians are automatically generated every 5 seconds to generate money for you passively.


In the "buildings" tab you can buy different buildings that have special bonuses to grow your empire.


To spend your hard earned money, head over to the "crates" tab in which you can spend money on crates to gain different special bonuses across the game!
