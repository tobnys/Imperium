You have now chosen an empire, press the economics tab to start earning!


Civilians are automatically generated every 5 seconds to generate money for you passively.


In the "buildings" tab you can preserve your workers to work in different buildings, doing this will reserve your workers to gain revenue in said building instead!


To spend your hard earned money, head over to the "crates" tab in which you can spend money on crates to gain different bonuses across the game!
